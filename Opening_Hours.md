# General Opening Hours for Tokyo Locations

**Note:** These are general opening hours. Hours may vary on weekends, holidays (especially late December/New Year), and specific dates. Please check the official websites for the most up-to-date information.

## 1. Sensoji Temple (Asakusa)
*   **Main Hall (Hondo):** 06:00 - 17:00 (April - September), 06:30 - 17:00 (October - March).
*   **Temple Grounds:** Always open (24 hours).
*   **Nakamise Shopping Street:** Shops generally open from **10:00 to 17:00**. Some may open earlier or close later.

## 2. Radio Kaikan Akihabara
*   **Hours:** 10:00 - 20:00 (Daily).

## 3. Mandarake Complex Akihabara
*   **Hours:** 12:00 - 20:00 (Daily).

## 4. Hey (Hirose Entertainment Yard) Akihabara
*   **Hours:** 10:00 - 23:30 (Daily).

## 5. @Home Cafe Akihabara (Main Store)
*   **Weekdays:** 11:00 - 22:00
*   **Weekends & Holidays:** 10:00 - 22:00
*   *Note: Last order is usually 30-60 minutes before closing.*

## 6. Gyukatsu Motomura (Akihabara / Ikebukuro)
*   **General Hours:** 11:00 - 22:00 (Last order around 21:00 - 21:30).
*   *Note: Very popular, expect queues.*

## 7. Pokemon Center Mega Tokyo (Sunshine City)
*   **Hours:** 10:00 - 20:00 (Daily).
*   *Located in Sunshine City Alpa 2F.*

## 8. Harajuku Takeshita Street
*   **General Shop Hours:** Most shops open around **10:00 - 11:00** and close around **19:00 - 20:00**.
*   *Note: It is a street, so individual shop hours vary.*

## 9. Shibuya Sky
*   **Hours:** 10:00 - 22:30 (Last admission 21:20).
*   *Note: Advance reservation is highly recommended, especially for sunset slots.*

## 10. Shibuya Parco (Nintendo Tokyo / Pokemon Center Shibuya)
*   **Shops (including Nintendo Tokyo):** 11:00 - 21:00.
*   **Restaurants (Chaos Kitchen):** 11:30 - 23:00.

## 11. Miyashita Park (Shibuya)
*   **Shops (Rayard Miyashita Park):** 11:00 - 21:00.
*   **Restaurants:** 11:00 - 23:00.
*   **Park (Rooftop):** 08:00 - 23:00.

## 12. Hikiniku to Come (Shibuya)
*   **Lunch:** 11:00 - 15:00
*   **Dinner:** 17:00 - 21:00
*   *Note: Requires a ticket (distributed in the morning) or online reservation. Often sells out early.*

## 13. Jojoen (Shibuya / Ikebukuro)
*   **General Hours:** 11:00 - 22:00 (or later, some locations open until 23:00 or midnight).
*   **Lunch Menu:** Usually available 11:00 - 15:00 or 16:00.

## 14. Fuji-Q Highland
*   **General Hours:** 09:00 - 17:00 or 18:00 (Weekdays), 09:00 - 18:00 or 19:00 (Weekends).
*   *Note: Hours vary significantly by season and day. Check the official calendar.*

## 15. Tokyo Disneyland & DisneySea
*   **General December Hours:** 09:00 - 21:00.
*   *Note: Opening times can be earlier (e.g., 08:15 or 08:30) for actual entry, even if officially 09:00. Check the official app/website.*
