# Gyoda Tourist Attractions & Food Guide (December Focus)

Here is the detailed information for your visit to Gyoda, Saitama, focusing on the areas near "Everyday Gyoda".

## 1. Ancient Lotus Park (Kodai Hasu no Sato / 古代蓮の里)
**Status:** **Highly Recommended for Winter Evening**
*   **Winter Illumination:** Yes! The park hosts the "Gyoda Uki-shiro no Sato Illumination" (行田「浮き城の里」あかり).
    *   **Period:** Typically early December to early January.
    *   **Time:** Sunset to around 21:00 or 21:30.
    *   **Highlights:** Massive LED displays, often themed around the "Rice Paddy Art" or local legends. The Observation Tower is usually open late on weekends during this period to view the lights from above.
*   **Opening Hours (Ancient Lotus Hall / Observation Tower):**
    *   **Regular:** 9:00 - 16:30 (Last entry 16:00).
    *   **During Illumination:** Often extended on weekends/holidays until 20:30 or 21:00.
    *   **Park Grounds:** Open 24 hours (Free entry).
*   **Description:** Famous for 1400-year-old lotus seeds that sprouted naturally. In winter, the lack of flowers is made up for by the spectacular illumination.
*   **Distance:** ~10 mins drive from Everyday Gyoda.

## 2. Oshi Castle (Oshi-jo / 忍城)
**Status:** **Recommended for History/Photos**
*   **Opening Hours (Gyoda City Provincial Museum):**
    *   **Hours:** 9:00 - 16:30 (Last entry 16:00).
    *   **Closed:** Mondays (open if holiday), the day after holidays, and **Dec 28 - Jan 4** (Year-end/New Year).
    *   **Castle Grounds:** Open 24 hours (Free).
*   **Description:** Known as the "Floating Castle" because it withstood a water siege by Ishida Mitsunari in 1590. The current structure is a reconstruction.
*   **Winter Visit:** The grounds are beautiful for photos. The museum inside explains the history of the siege and the castle town.
*   **Distance:** ~10-15 mins drive from Everyday Gyoda.

## 3. Sakitama Kofun Park (Sakitama Ancient Burial Mounds / さきたま古墳公園)
**Status:** **Good for a Walk (Weather Permitting)**
*   **Opening Hours (Museum of the Sakitama Ancient Burial Mounds):**
    *   **Hours:** 9:00 - 16:30 (Last entry 16:00).
    *   **Closed:** Mondays and **Dec 29 - Jan 3**.
*   **Park Grounds:** Open 24 hours (Free).
*   **Description:** A National Special Historic Site with 9 large keyhole-shaped burial mounds (Kofun) from the 5th-7th centuries.
*   **Winter Visit:** It can be very windy ("Karakaze" dry wind). Dress warmly. Climbing the "Maruhakayama Kofun" (Japan's largest circular tomb) offers a great view, sometimes seeing Mt. Fuji on clear winter days.
*   **Distance:** ~10 mins drive from Everyday Gyoda.

## 4. Food Spots: "Jelly Fry" (Zeny-furai / ゼリーフライ)
**What is it?** A croquette made of potatoes and okara (soy pulp), without breadcrumbs. It's shaped like a koban (oval coin) and dipped in a savory sauce. The name comes from "Zeni-furai" (Coin Fry) -> "Zelly Fry".

**Recommended Spots:**

1.  **Komagata-ya (駒形屋)**
    *   **Description:** A classic, nostalgic shop often cited as one of the best.
    *   **Hours:** 10:00 - 16:00 (Closed Mondays).
    *   **Location:** Near Sakitama Kofun Park.

2.  **Kanehachi (かねはち)**
    *   **Description:** Located near the Chichibu Railway Gyodashi Station. Famous for its soup-infused Jelly Fry.
    *   **Hours:** 11:00 - 14:00 (Check locally, often closes when sold out).

3.  **Kodai Hasu no Sato (Snack Shop)**
    *   **Description:** There is usually a food stand or shop within the Ancient Lotus Park that sells Jelly Fry. Convenient if you are visiting for the illumination.
    *   **Hours:** Varies, usually closes around 16:00 unless extended for events.

4.  **Oshi Castle (Tourism Center)**
    *   **Description:** Sometimes available at the tourism center or nearby shops.

**Note:** Many Jelly Fry shops are small, family-run places that may close early or have irregular holidays in late December.

## Summary for December Visit
*   **Afternoon:** Play at **Everyday Gyoda**.
*   **Late Afternoon:** Visit **Oshi Castle** for photos (Museum closes 16:00).
*   **Sunset/Evening:** Go to **Ancient Lotus Park** for the **Illumination**.
*   **Food:** Grab **Jelly Fry** at a shop near the Kofun Park or at the Lotus Park before it gets too late.
