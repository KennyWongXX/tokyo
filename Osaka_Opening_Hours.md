# General Opening Hours for Osaka Locations

**Note:** These are general opening hours. Hours may vary on weekends, holidays (especially late December/New Year), and specific dates. Please check the official websites for the most up-to-date information.

## 1. Kuromon Market (General shops)
*   **General Hours:** 09:00 - 18:00.
*   *Note: Individual shop hours vary. Some open earlier for breakfast, some close later.*

## 2. Round 1 Sennichimae
*   **Hours:** 24 Hours (Daily).
*   *Note: Amusement corners may have restrictions for minors late at night.*

## 3. Parco Shinsaibashi
*   **Shops:** 10:00 - 20:00.
*   **Restaurants:** 11:00 - 23:00.
*   *Note: Some specific stores or floors may have different hours.*

## 4. Mizuno (Okonomiyaki) Dotonbori
*   **Hours:** 11:00 - 22:00.
*   *Note: Popular spot, expect queues.*

## 5. Acchichi Honpo (Takoyaki) Dotonbori
*   **Hours:** 09:00 - 02:00 (Next day).
*   *Note: Located by the river, often has a line.*

## 6. Universal Studios Japan (USJ)
*   **General December Hours:** 08:30 - 21:00 (or 20:00).
*   **Dec 31:** 09:00 - 17:00 (Regular hours), followed by Countdown Party (Ticket required).
*   *Note: Opening times can be earlier than officially stated. Check the official app.*

## 7. Kinryu Ramen (Dotonbori)
*   **Hours:** 24 Hours (Daily).

## 8. Super Kids Land (Den Den Town)
*   **Main Store:** 10:00 - 20:00.
*   **Character Hall:** 10:00 - 20:00.

## 9. Animate (Den Den Town/Nipponbashi)
*   **Hours:** 11:00 - 21:00 (Weekdays), 10:00 - 20:00 (Weekends/Holidays).

## 10. Tsutenkaku Tower
*   **General Observation Deck:** 10:00 - 20:00 (Last entry 19:30).
*   **Special Outdoors Deck:** 10:00 - 19:50 (Last entry 19:30).

## 11. Kushikatsu Daruma (Shinsekai Main Store)
*   **Hours:** 11:00 - 22:30 (Last order 22:00).

## 12. Jumbo Fishing Boat Tea House (Shinsekai/Namba)
*   **Hours:** 11:00 - 23:00 (Weekends/Holidays), 17:00 - 23:00 (Weekdays - Namba).
*   *Note: Check specific store (Shinsekai vs Namba) as weekday lunch might vary.*

## 13. Expocity
*   **NIFREL:** 10:00 - 18:00 (Last admission 17:00).
*   **VS PARK:** 10:00 - 21:00 (Last entry 19:00).
*   **Lalaport Expocity (Shops):** 10:00 - 20:00 (Weekdays), 10:00 - 21:00 (Weekends).

## 14. Nara Park (Todaiji Temple)
*   **Todaiji (Daibutsuden):** 08:00 - 17:00 (November - March).
*   **Nara Park:** Open 24 Hours (Deer are active during the day).
